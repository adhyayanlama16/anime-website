The Future Of Indian Animation


Anime is a Japanese hand-drawn or computer animation.
The word abbreviated pronunciation "animation" in Japanese where this terms references all animation. Outside Japan, anime is used to refer specifically to animation style often characterized by colorful graphics, vibrant characters, and fantastical themes.
Arguable, the culturally abstract approach to the word's meaning may open up the possibility of anime produced in countries other than Japan. For simplicity, many Westerners strictly view anime as a Japanese animation product. Some scholars suggest defining anime as specifically or quintessentially Japanese may be related to a new form of orientalism.


Famous Indian Animators

R.G BUCKET LIST


Rohit Gogoi, better known online as R.G Bucket List (also written as RG Bucket List) is an Indian animator and storytime YouTuber who uploads animated vlog videos to YouTube. He often live-streams videos on YouTube as updates while working for newer videos. The videos on this channel mostly revolve around adolescent experiences and relatable situations.

KIRTI CHOW


Kirti Choudhury, (born: August 11, 1999 [age 21]) better known online as KirtiChow, is an Indian vlogger who makes animation vlogs and videos on YouTube. In his videos, he appears as an animated boy from the torso-up with a light blue T-Shirt with a 'K' on it. He learned how to animate on YouTube itself and posted his first public video on 2nd April, 2018, a month after starting the channel. He makes videos on his personal experience, tells stories, and earlier used to make anime-styled short stories.

ANGRY PRASH


Prashant a.k.a Angry Prash is an Indian YouTube star who primarily posts comedic animated content. He has amassed over 4.34 million subscribers on his YouTube channel. Masked behind an animatic headgear, Prashant is known for his humorous and quirky content on the video-sharing platform.

YOGI BABA


YOGI BABA is an Indian YouTube channel with over 500k+ subscribers and having more than 70+million views and still increasing rapidly. Yogi baba is owned by Anuj Sahay, Yogesh Sharma and Rajeev. The channel is very much popular for its comedy and rant videos. The most popular series in this youtube channel is their nutshell videos.

PUFF TALKS


PUFF TALKS is an Indian YouTube channel with over 80k+ subscribers and having more than 44lakhs+ views and still increasing rapidly. Pink guy from India The channel is very much popular for its comedy videos.
